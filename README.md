## 👋 About Me

I'm a **5th-year Computer Engineering student** at **Egypt University of Informatics (EUI)**, passionate about pushing the limits of deep learning — from building generative models from scratch to fine-tuning large vision-language models on TPU clusters.

- 🔬 I work extensively with **GANs, Diffusion Models, VAEs, Vision Transformers**, and **Contrastive Learning**
- 🧠 I've built and trained models end-to-end: **Latent Diffusion (Text-to-Image on ImageNet 1.2M)**, **Brain Tumor Segmentation with CLIP-conditioned DDIM**, **TransUNet Medical Segmentation (80% Jaccard)**
- ⚡ Comfortable with **distributed training** on multi-GPU and multi-TPU setups
- 🎯 Into **model optimization**: LoRA, QLoRA, DoRA, Quantization, Pruning, ONNX, TensorRT
- 🛠️ I contribute to open-source, including **keras-hub** model porting (CNNs, PANNs)
- 📍 Based in Egypt — building things that matter
